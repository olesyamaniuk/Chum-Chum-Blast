About the Game

  • Chum Chum Blast is a fun and engaging puzzle game that challenges players to tap, match, and blast their way through vibrant and dynamic levels. The game offers a unique blend of strategic gameplay and exciting mechanics that keep players entertained for hours. Each level presents a fresh challenge, requiring quick thinking and clever use of boosters to progress.
  • As you advance through the game, you'll encounter an array of delightful Chum Chum characters who bring life to the adventure. These friendly companions guide you through different levels, offering helpful tips and unlocking new abilities along the way. Completing levels not only rewards you with points but also grants access to special events, time-limited challenges, and exclusive in-game prizes.
  • Chum Chum Blast is designed for players of all skill levels, offering a smooth learning curve with increasingly complex puzzles. Whether you prefer a casual gaming experience or a more competitive challenge, this game provides something for everyone. With frequent updates, fresh content, and exciting rewards, there is always something new to discover in the world of Chum Chum Blast. Get ready to dive into a colorful, energetic, and rewarding puzzle adventure like no other!

How to Play

  • Tap to Blast Blocks:

    Simply tap two or more adjacent blocks of the same color to blast them away. The more blocks you clear in one move, the bigger the combo and the greater your score!

  • Use Boosters to Overcome Challenges:

    Activate powerful boosters like bombs, rockets, and color blasters to clear obstacles and achieve your objectives faster. Combining boosters creates spectacular chain reactions for massive rewards!

  • Complete Level Objectives:

    Each level comes with unique goals, such as collecting specific items, reaching a target score, or clearing obstacles. Plan your moves strategically to maximize efficiency and earn stars!

  • Unlock Exciting Power-Ups:

    As you progress, discover and unlock new power-ups that help you tackle even the toughest puzzles. Strategically use them to gain an advantage and blast your way through the most challenging levels.

  • Engage in Special Events and Challenges:

    Participate in time-limited events and daily challenges to earn exclusive rewards. These special events introduce fresh gameplay mechanics and keep the experience exciting!

  • Connect with Friends and Compete on Leaderboards:

    Track your progress, challenge your friends, and climb the leaderboards to showcase your puzzle-solving skills. Play with friends and see who can achieve the highest scores!
